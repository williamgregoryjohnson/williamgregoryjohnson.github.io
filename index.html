I am a PhD candidate and Graduate Teaching Assistant in the Department of Computer Science, Georgia State University.

CV, BIO

Currently, I am under advisement with Dr. Anu G. Bourgeois.

In 2004, I completed my Masters of Science in Computer Science at Georgia State University Department of Computer Science under the supervision of Dr. Sushil Prasad.

My research interests are in Computer Science Education Research for undergraduate students. In particular my research focuses on the learning biases in computer science first degree students through using targeted educational big data mining techniques with a goal to increase program retention and successful graduation pathways for minorities. Also, I am a Fellow in the Neuroscience Institute, Brains and Behavior at GSU.
